# Functional and Non-Functional Requirements

## Functional Requirements

### 1. Inventory Management

- The system must allow adding new products to the inventory.
- It must allow updating product stock levels.
- It must record product expiration dates.
- It must allow removal of obsolete or expired products.

### 2. Sales Registration

- The system must allow selling products to customers.
- It must automatically calculate the total purchase price.
- It must record customer information (name, address, contact number) for prescription sales.
- It must generate a receipt for each sale.

### 3. Product Search and Query

- It must allow quick product search by name, code, or category.
- It must provide detailed information for each product, including price, stock, and expiration date.

### 4. Customer Management

- It must allow creating and maintaining customer records.
- It must provide information about customers' previous purchases.
- It must allow assigning discounts or loyalty programs to regular customers.
- Customers must have a `clienteamigo` number to access our discount program.

### 5. Report Generation

- It must be able to generate daily, weekly, monthly, and annual sales reports.
- It must provide up-to-date inventory reports.

### 6. Category Management

- The system includes a category module with predefined and custom types.
- Custom categories can be submitted for approval or rejection.
- Active categories are available in product forms and inventory filters.

## Non-Functional Requirements

### 1. Intuitive User Interface

- The interface must be simple, clear, and easy to use.
- The system must allow quick identification of inventory, sales, customer, and report actions.
